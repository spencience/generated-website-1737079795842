About the EP

Midnight Vibes EP represents a journey through the nocturnal soundscape of modern electronic music. Each track captures a different moment of the night, from the energetic beginning to the peaceful approach of dawn.

Produced during late-night sessions in Eddie's Tokyo studio, this EP combines classic deep house elements with modern production techniques, creating a unique sonic experience that's both nostalgic and forward-thinking.
