About Eddie Platinum

From underground warehouse raves to sold-out arena shows, DJ Eddie Platinum has been pushing the boundaries of electronic music for over a decade.

Background

Born in Detroit's electronic music scene, Eddie discovered his passion for mixing tracks in his parent's garage. His unique blend of house, techno, and progressive elements has earned him recognition as one of the most innovative producers in the industry.

Philosophy

"Music is more than just sound - it's a journey that connects people across cultures and boundaries. Every set is an opportunity to create something magical." - Eddie Platinum

Career Highlights

2020

Grammy nomination for "Best Electronic Album" - Platinum Dreams

2021

Headlined major festivals including Tomorrowland and Ultra Music Festival

2023

Launched Platinum Records label and world tour

Current Projects

Eddie is currently working on his highly anticipated third studio album while maintaining residencies in Ibiza and Las Vegas. His weekly radio show "Platinum Hours" reaches millions of listeners worldwide.
